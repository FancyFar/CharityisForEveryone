WHO WE ARE

We are a non- profit organisation that helps different groups of people who are in need of support in Singapore. We believe that Charity can be done by everyone and that anyone can participate. This organisation was founded to encourage more people in the Singapore community to lend a helping hand to others in need, to build a more compassionate and inclusive society as well as to support the underprivileged.
